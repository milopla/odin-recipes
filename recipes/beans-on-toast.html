Beans on toast

This is a quick and simple recipe for making beans on toast, a British culinary staple.

Ingredients:

  • Sliced bread
  • Salted butter
  • Baked beans
  1. Toast two slices of bread in the toaster until golden brown.
  2. Whilst toasting, microwave the baked beans until piping hot.
  3. Butter the toast, and top with a generous layer of baked beans.
