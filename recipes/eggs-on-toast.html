Eggs on toast

This is a quick and simple recipe for making eggs on toast: a simple, hearty meal that will fill you right up!

Ingredients:

  • Sliced bread
  • Salted butter
  • 2 eggs
  1. Toast two slices of bread in the toaster until golden brown.
  2. Whilst toasting, add butter to a pan, crack two eggs, and fry until your preferred doneness.
  3. Butter the toast, and top with those delicious eggs.
